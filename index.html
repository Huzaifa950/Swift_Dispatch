How to Get Started

1. MC Authority

A copy of your Motor Carrier Authority

2. CERTIFICATE OF INSURANCE

It must reflect $1,000,000 Liability and $100,000 Cargo.

3. W-9 FORM

A copy of your W-9 or IRS assigned EIN number.

4. Notice of Assignment (NoA)

It's a document you receive from your factoring company.

5. DRIVER & TRUCK IDENTIFICATION

Driver License and Truck's License Plate.

6. DISPATCH SERVICE AGREEMENT

Complete Online or Contact us.
